International Express Mail Service (IEMS)


International Express Mail Service (IEMS) is the fastest means of sending time-sensitive items to addresses abroad. Any articles such as documents, samples of merchandise and goods, not specifically prohibited by regulations, can be sent via IEMS. Currently, the Philippines has a bilateral agreement with 45 countries for the reciprocal exchange of EMS items.

Domestic Express Mail Service (DEMS)


Domestic Express Mail Service (DEMS) is the fastest means of sending urgent messages, business documents or goods to addresses within the Philippines. Generally, DEMS items are delivered the next day after the date of mailing provided that they are posted on or before the prescribed cut-off time. Currently, the service is available in all major cities and towns nationwide.

Express Pouch


Express Pouch is a next day delivery guaranteed to time-sensitive and important documents. It is a guaranteed next-day door-to-door delivery to selected areas either by land or air.
